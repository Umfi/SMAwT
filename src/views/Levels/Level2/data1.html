Digital Footprint

Social media is a great way to communicate with others and express yourself. You can also use it to show others, including colleges or future employers, who you are as a person. However, social media use can also have drawbacks. What you and others share is permanent, and it's easy to get caught in sharing things you may later regret.

Your digital footprint can affect your reputation online and offline. It's important to manage it as much as you can!
